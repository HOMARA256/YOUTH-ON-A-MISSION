Few Words About Us

YOUTH ON A MISSION is a Youth led Community Based Organization formed by the youths of Abim district who share the common economic hardships like unemployment, hunger, poverty and lack of involvement in decision making in their community among other domestic challenges in their daily lives.,

The idea of forming this was conceived when some youths realized that they could not find solutions to their problems individually without uniting and working together.

YOUTH ON A MISSION is a non-political, non-religious and nonsectarian Community Based organization whose activities aims to champion youth involvement in agriculture to fight food insecurity in KARAMOJA, change the negative mindset of the youth through mentorship and career guidance, financial training to instill saving and investment culture among the youths by carrying out weekly savings, sensitizing the youths about the dangers of HIV/AIDS, alcoholism, teenage pregnancy and domestic violence

VISION

A healthy and prosperous youth population, who are actively engaged in the affairs of their society and acting as role model for the future generation.

MISSION

To empower the youth economically with financial knowledge, improving household food security through actively engaging in commercial agriculture and provide opportunity for the youth to actively engage in decision making and the affairs of their community and Karamoja at large.
